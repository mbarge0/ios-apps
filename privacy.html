Last Updated: March 15, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all iOS applications published by Matthew Barge ("Developer"). This policy describes how apps may collect, use, and share information.

Depending on the app, we may collect:

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service. You can delete your data by uninstalling the app or contacting us.

5. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
